Load Forecasting & Demand Planning

Use AI to predict future energy demand so you can plan production, capacity and contracts with confidence.

What this use case does

  • Analyses historical and real‑time energy data to forecast demand at plant, building or feeder level.
  • Helps schedule production and maintenance around expected peaks and troughs.
  • Supports better negotiations for power purchase and demand‑based tariffs.

Typical benefits

  • Fewer surprises from sudden load spikes or bottlenecks.
  • Lower energy costs by avoiding peak penalties and oversizing.
  • More stable operations thanks to better planning and coordination.
