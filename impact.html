A federated data mesh architecture is a modern approach to data management that emphasizes decentralization, domain ownership and self-service. It allows organizations to scale their data infrastructure and analytics capabilities while maintaining agility, flexibility and innovation. By adopting a federated data mesh architecture, companies can break down data silos, empower teams to own their data products and pipelines, and foster a culture of collaboration and experimentation.

  • Federated Data Mesh Architecture organizes data by business domains instead of one central data lake
  • Each domain owns its data products and pipelines but follows common standards for quality, security and governance
  • This keeps data distributed and scalable while still making it easy to share, discover and reuse across the company
  • Keep datasets, infrastructure and logs consistent and traceable.
  • Integrate cybersecurity, data governance and fraud detection into every layer.
