Asset Performance & Reliability Analytics

Track the health and efficiency of critical assets to improve reliability and plan upgrades with data, not guesswork.

What this use case does

  • Collects performance and fault data from assets like transformers, inverters, motors and compressors.
  • Builds KPIs such as availability, mean time between failures and efficiency for each asset.
  • Highlights underperforming assets, locations or lines that need attention.

Typical benefits

  • Higher uptime and fewer unexpected breakdowns.
  • Better planning of replacements, refurbishments and spares.
  • Clear justification for capex based on real performance data.
