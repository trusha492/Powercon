Anomaly & Fraud Detection in Energy Usage

Use AI to spot unusual energy consumption that may indicate leaks, theft, faulty meters or control issues.

What this use case does

  • Monitors load profiles at meter, feeder, building or equipment level in near real‑time.
  • Flags abnormal patterns compared to historical behaviour or similar assets.
  • Groups and prioritises suspected anomalies for investigation by your team.

Typical benefits

  • Early detection of leaks, wiring issues or misconfigured equipment.
  • Reduced commercial losses from energy theft or incorrect metering.
  • Better trust in your metering and billing data for future analytics.
